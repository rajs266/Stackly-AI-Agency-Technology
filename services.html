FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our services? Find quick answers below.

What type of models do you support?
We work with standard open-weight architectures (Llama, Mistral), custom vision models (YOLO), transformer systems, and custom reinforcement layers built in PyTorch.
Do we retain ownership of the trained weights?
Yes, absolutely. All fine-tuned models, datasets, weights, and application codebase remain 100% your proprietary IP.
Can you deploy AI models directly on our local servers?
Yes. Under our Enterprise tier, we build secure local Docker clusters that keep your calculations entirely inside your corporate LAN framework.
What is the typical timeline for an AI implementation project?
A standard custom AI model integration goes from discovery to deployment in 4 to 8 weeks, depending on data cleanliness.
How do you handle data privacy and security?
We encrypt all client telemetry datasets during transfer and rest, following strict ISO and GDPR data compliance guidelines.
